The Challenge: High Ad Costs

The owner of Dial An Appliance Man is an expert at fixing home appliances. He has a great team and happy customers, but online, he was invisible to the local community in Edmonton.

He was caught in a cycle of high-cost advertising. Every lead came from Google Ads, which meant his profit margins were being eaten by "Cost-Per-Click." If he stopped paying Google, the phone stopped ringing.

Our goal was to apply a technical SEO audit to fix the foundation of his site, allowing it to rank naturally and build long-term authority.

The Engineering Solution

Using the same technical rigor I apply to industrial data systems, I performed a **Deep Site Crawl**. We identified massive technical debt: slow loading speeds on mobile and broken metadata that confused Google's bots.

I didn't just add keywords; I restructured the site's architecture. I ensured every service—from fridge repair to dryer maintenance—had a high-performance landing page that Google could trust.

To further enhance local trust, I utilized **Advanced AI tools** to create high-resolution, professional imagery. This didn't just help SEO; it increased the conversion rate of visitors into paying customers.
