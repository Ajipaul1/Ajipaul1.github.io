Building Organic Visibility for a Local Appliance Repair Business

Industry: Home Services / Repair
Location: Canada
Focus: Technical SEO & Structure

The Business

The client is a local appliance repair company serving residential customers. Their technicians were experienced and trusted offline, but online visibility relied almost entirely on paid Google Ads.

They had the service quality, but their digital foundation was weak.

The Problem

Although the business had strong service quality, their website did not clearly communicate services to search engines.

We identified critical gaps in the website's technical architecture:

  • Unclear service page hierarchy
  • Missing local intent signals
  • Technical SEO issues confusing crawlers
  • Keyword cannibalization (pages competing with each other)

What We Changed
