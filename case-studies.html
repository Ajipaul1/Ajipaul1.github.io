🛒 E-commerce SEO

205% Organic Growth for Home Appliance Retailer — Canadian Market

A comprehensive technical SEO intervention and content architecture rebuild for a Canadian home appliance retail business — resolving structural crawl issues, eliminating keyword cannibalization, and engineering a local SEO foundation that reduced their dependency on paid advertising.

🌍 Canada 📅 2023–2024 🔧 Technical SEO 📈 205% Organic Growth
📊 Key Results

Top 3
Local Keyword Rankings

Ad Spend Increase
🔍 Diagnosed Problems
Keyword Cannibalization

Multiple pages targeting the same appliance service keywords were competing against each other, splitting ranking signals and preventing any single page from performing.

Unclear Service Hierarchy

Search engines couldn't determine which service category pages were primary — no clear crawl path from root to individual service offerings.

Missing Local Intent Signals

Pages lacked geo-specific content, LocalBusiness schema, and NAP consistency — making it impossible to rank for "[city] + appliance repair" queries.

Crawl Waste & Thin Content

Hundreds of near-duplicate pages were consuming crawl budget without contributing to search visibility, diluting the site's overall authority.

✅ What We Fixed
  • 🏗️
    Service Silo ArchitectureRebuilt the entire site structure around clean service categories — each appliance type as a distinct silo with its own canonical URL, optimized heading hierarchy, and unique content.
  • 📍
    Local SEO EngineeringDeployed LocalBusiness schema with correct service areas, NAP data, and geo-coordinates. Created city-specific landing pages for top service markets in Canada.
  • 🔗
    Internal Link RedesignMapped a new internal linking structure to flow PageRank toward priority service pages — eliminating orphaned pages and reinforcing topic clusters.
  • 🤖
    Crawl Budget OptimizationConsolidated thin content pages via 301 redirects, added noindex tags to utility pages, and rebuilt robots.txt to direct crawler resources toward revenue-generating service pages.
  • 📊
    Search Console MonitoringSet up coverage reporting, index request workflows, and monthly crawl re-audits to catch and resolve new issues before they impact rankings.
📈 Why It Worked
Engineering Rigor

We treated the site as a technical system — diagnosing root causes rather than applying surface-level keyword fixes.

No Ad Dependency Growth

The entire 205% traffic increase came from organic — proving that structural SEO done correctly outperforms ad spend in the long run.

Search Intent Alignment

Every page was mapped to a specific user intent, ensuring visitors found exactly what they needed — reducing bounce rates and increasing time on page.

Sustained Results

Unlike PPC which stops the moment you stop paying, the structural improvements compound over time, continuing to drive traffic months after implementation.
