Technical SEO Case Analysis

Client: Dial An Appliance Man (Canada) | Duration: 90 Days

Project Overview

Dial An Appliance Man

Company Profile: A leading multi-city appliance repair service provider in Alberta, Canada (Calgary, Airdrie, Spruce Grove).

The Challenge: Despite high service quality, the company was "digitally invisible" for key search terms. They were stuck on Page 2 & 3 of Google, causing a heavy reliance on high-cost paid advertising.

Fig 1.1: Pre-optimization audit showing 0 high-intent keywords in Top 10 positions.

Competitor Gap Analysis

I conducted a technical reverse-engineering of 888 competitors. I identified that rivals like mapleleafappliances.ca were winning due to superior local citation density and "Trust Flow" backlinks.

Fig 2.1: Semantic Keyword Gap Analysis identifying missed opportunities in local Airdrie markets.

Implementation
AI-Powered

Using an engineering-first mindset, I implemented a three-tier "SEO 2.0" solution:

  1. AI Landing Pages: Generated city-specific descriptions for 10+ service areas using custom LLM prompts.
  2. Visual Asset Synthesis: Replaced low-res stock photos with high-end, AI-generated imagery to boost user trust (CTR).
  3. Social Mapping: Established consistent multi-city social profiles to improve "NAP" (Name, Address, Phone) consistency.
