Liquid Template Tuning & Collection Routing Canonicalization

High-ranking online stores require clean, optimized templates. While Shopify offers a user-friendly admin panel, its default theme setups often create search crawl traps. The platform generates duplicate product URLs depending on how users access them (e.g. through specific collection pages), which can cause canonical conflicts and dilute PageRank value.

At TechAuditPros, a technical shopify seo company founded by Aji Paul in Kochi, Kerala, we resolve these structural challenges. We edit your Liquid theme files (modifying product.liquid templates) to ensure all internal links point directly to primary product URLs, consolidating your search authority.

We analyze how your theme handles dynamic pages. We modify robots.txt configurations to block search crawlers from index-bloating dynamic filter pages, keeping search bot attention focused on primary categories.

Microdata Schemas & Asset Caching Diagnostics

Implementing structured JSON-LD schemas is key to standing out in search results. We write custom schemas directly into your Liquid templates, pulling aggregate ratings, price ranges, and inventory status to display rich snippets across searches.

This database-driven approach prevents markup mismatches, securing rich product snippet features like stars, price tags, and inventory indicators across organic results.

Additionally, we optimize loading speeds. We audit Liquid loops, lazy-load images, compress heavy media assets, defer non-critical JS scripts, and audit third-party apps that slow down Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) speeds.
