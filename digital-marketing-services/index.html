Digital Marketing Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our digital marketing company workflows, budgets, and expectations.

What is digital marketing? ▼

Digital marketing encompasses all promotional efforts that use an electronic device or the internet. Businesses leverage digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites to connect with current and prospective customers.

How much does digital marketing cost in India? ▼

The cost of digital marketing services varies greatly depending on project scale. Small local campaigns can cost between ₹15,000 to ₹30,000 monthly, while comprehensive enterprise campaigns targeting international markets like the USA and UK can range from ₹75,000 to ₹3,00,000+ per month depending on ad spend requirements.

What is the ROI of digital marketing? ▼

Return on investment (ROI) in digital marketing is highly measurable. Paid ad campaigns (PPC) can target a specific cost-per-lead, while organic content and technical SEO strategies build long-term value, compounding organic clicks over time with zero media costs.

What is SEO in digital marketing? ▼

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website to increase its visibility when people search for products or services related to your business in search engines. Greater visibility leads to more attention and attracts prospective customers.

What is PPC in digital marketing? ▼

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is a model of digital advertising where advertisers pay a fee each time one of their ads is clicked. Essentially, it is a way of buying visits to your site, rather than attempting to "earn" those visits organically.

How long does digital marketing take to show results? ▼

Paid advertising (Google and Meta Ads) can drive immediate traffic and leads within 24 to 48 hours of campaign launch. Technical SEO and content marketing strategy generally take between 3 to 6 months to demonstrate compounding organic keyword ranking results.
