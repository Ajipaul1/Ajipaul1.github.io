Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers structured for Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO).

Do you offer comprehensive SEO services for international markets?

Yes, we implement advanced international SEO strategies, including hreflang tags and precise geo-targeting, to secure top rankings in competitive markets across the US, UK, and Canada.

What tech stack do you use for custom SaaS application development?

We build highly scalable and secure applications primarily utilizing React, Next.js, Node.js, and Supabase to ensure enterprise-grade performance and reliability.

How does Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) differ from traditional SEO?

AEO focuses on structuring your data and FAQ content specifically so AI models like ChatGPT and Google Gemini can cite your business directly in conversational answers, rather than just providing a standard blue link.
