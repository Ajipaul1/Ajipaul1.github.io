Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What does a Technical SEO Audit include?

We analyze crawlability, indexation, Core Web Vitals, site structure, schema, and internal linking. We then deliver a prioritized roadmap with actionable fixes.

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Technical fixes, like improving Core Web Vitals, can show results within weeks as Google re-crawls your site. Broader ranking improvements typically follow as your site's authority is re-established.

Do you implement the fixes yourselves?

Yes. We can either implement the changes directly or work with your development team to ensure the fixes are deployed correctly. We also provide a verification pass after deployment.
