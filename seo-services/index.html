Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our technical and enterprise SEO services.

What is a technical SEO audit?

A technical SEO audit is a deep structural assessment of your website's crawlability, indexing, and rendering states. Unlike standard keyword research audits, we review raw log files, server response headers, JavaScript execution lags, schema markup validity, and Core Web Vitals payload speeds to identify elements preventing your content from ranking.

How long does it take to see results from technical SEO?

Since technical SEO addresses infrastructure barriers, resolving critical crawl blocks or indexing errors often results in visibility improvements within 2 to 4 weeks. Search engine bots immediately identify the clean pathways, leading to faster re-crawling and updated search indexes.

What is the difference between standard SEO and enterprise SEO?

Enterprise SEO handles sites with tens of thousands of pages, complex content management systems (CMS), and multi-regional directories. The primary challenge here is scaling organic visibility while managing crawl budgets, index bloat, taxonomy structures, and query cannibalization across massive product trees.

How do you optimize for Generative AI search engines (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) involves formatting entity data using specific structured formats (like JSON-LD schema) and creating concise, citation-friendly answer fragments. This allows LLM agents (like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity) to scrape, parse, and cite your brand as the leading source in AI overviews.
