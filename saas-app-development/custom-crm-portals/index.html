Custom CRM Portals & Onboarding Platforms

TechAuditPros is a technical SEO and custom software engineering agency headquartered in Kochi, Kerala, India, building bespoke CRM portals and onboarding platforms for clients in the US, Canada, UK, and India. Off-the-shelf CRM systems charge high licensing fees and force you to adapt your business processes to their layouts. We build custom software that fits your existing workflows.

Bespoke CRM Features Tailored for Growth

We develop client portal systems that simplify customer management and increase conversions. Key features of our custom setups include:

  • Automated Onboarding: Client-facing workflows that guide users through document uploads and profiles setup automatically.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Defining precise data access levels for sales reps, client accounts, managers, and external partners.
  • Real-Time Pipeline Tracking: Dynamic Kanban boards that track client opportunities throughout your sales cycle.

Engineered on Scalable Modern Frameworks

We build our portal applications using Next.js, Supabase, and PostgreSQL. This tech stack ensures fast loading speeds, low hosting costs, and high database reliability. By separating the user interface from your primary database, we create secure environments that protect customer data and scale to handle traffic spikes.

Designed and Managed in Kochi, Kerala

Under founder Aji Paul, our Kochi-based team ensures that your custom CRM integrates smoothly with your existing systems. We map out data flows, run security tests, and build clean dashboards. We focus on creating clean, intuitive user interfaces that reduce training time for your team and onboarding delays for your clients.
