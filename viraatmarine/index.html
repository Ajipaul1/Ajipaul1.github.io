A Legacy of Quality

A Journey of Vision, Growth & Excellence

From a small marine consultancy in 2017 to a premier shipbuilding and fabrication enterprise in 2026.

2017

Consultancy Foundations

Founded Viraat Marine as a marine consultancy. Specialized in vessel design, stability assessments, and preparing stability booklets, building early client trust in Kochi's naval sectors.

2019

Entering Steel Construction

Successfully undertook the hull fabrication of "Rainbow", marking the company's entry into metal vessel construction and expanding beyond design consultancy services.

2020

SILK Royal Tourism Project

Secured the labor contract for the complete construction of "Royal Tourism", a passenger vessel for SILK. Proved project management capabilities and technical construction skills.

2021

Incorporation & Taj Cruise

Incorporated as a Private Limited Company. Began construction of the prestigious "Taj Cruise", a luxury 2-bedroom houseboat that redefined backwater elegance.

2022

Portfolio Expansion

Commenced Falcon Cruise and Blue Whale II (a 16m cruise vessel). Completed the retrofit and modernization of Royal Tourism I, showcasing versatility in retrofits.

2023

Tourism Craftsmanship

Built Malabar Palace 2-bedroom houseboat, Vellikkeel Houseboat, and Black Pearl Shikkara, achieving high customer satisfaction in the tourism sector.

2025 - 2026

Leading Inland Cruise Builders

Established as a premier brand across India. Built Kochi Pandaw and Wave Rider (Club Mahindra) in 2025. Followed by Kerala Pandaw and the Aqua Castle houseboat in 2026.

Specialized Portfolios

Marine Engineering Capabilities

From lines plan calculations to specialized steel structures fabrication, we manage the complete lifecycle.

01 / ENGINEERING

Marine Design Services

Our marine design services focus on creating efficient and innovative solutions for various marine vessels and structures. We leverage advanced engineering principles and modernistic CAD/FEA technology to optimize stability analysis, structural lines, hull lines, propulsion integration, fuel efficiency, and structural safety to meet class standards.

02 / CRAFTSMANSHIP

Marine Fabrication

Construction and assembly of marine structures using specialized steel, aluminum, and composite materials. Our fabrication ranges from custom offshore equipment, platforms, and structural frames to custom marine retrofits, using precision CNC plasma profiling, advanced MIG/TIG welding, and stringent ISO quality assurance checks.

03 / SHIPBUILDING

Custom Boat Building

Specializing in luxury houseboats, custom speedboats, and passenger vessels. We build each hull to exact client specifications, ensuring safety, IRS-class compliance, and high durability.

04 / MANAGEMENT

Marine Project Management

Our project managers drive the lifecycle from scheduling, resource allocation, and risk management through to dry-docking, sea trials, and final commissioning. We guarantee on-time project delivery within budget while satisfying all safety regulations and stakeholder requirements.
