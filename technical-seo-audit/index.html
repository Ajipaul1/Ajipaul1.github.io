What Is a Technical SEO Audit?

Think of your website as a building. Before you paint the walls (content), you need to ensure the foundation is solid, the plumbing (redirects) works, and the electricity (server response) is reliable.

A technical SEO audit examines how search engines access, crawl, and interpret your website. Even strong content cannot rank if technical issues prevent search engines from understanding or trusting your infrastructure.

The Blueprint Diagnostics

Our audits analyze server-side code, headers, redirects, render speeds, and XML sitemaps to ensure search engine spiders can crawl your layout efficiently.

We resolve faceted navigation traps, indexing loops, render-blocking scripts, and duplicate paths that dilute PageRank authority.

What We Review

An inspection of the core pillars of search infrastructure.

🕷️

Crawlability

Ensuring search bots can discover all important pages without hitting dead ends, 404s, or robots.txt blockages.

📂

Indexing

Checking canonical tags, no-index directives, and URL parameter handling to ensure the right pages appear in search results.

⚡

Performance

Analyzing Core Web Vitals, server response times (TTFB), database speeds, and rendering pathways for better user signals.

🧱

Architecture

Reviewing URL structure, internal link patterns, schema formats, and parent-child site hierarchy to pass authority effectively.

Who Needs a Technical SEO Audit?

If your site experiences any of these symptoms, your underlying architecture is likely bottlenecked.

  • 📉 Unexplained Traffic Drops Sudden or steady declines in organic search visibility are often caused by indexing loops or code-level errors.
  • 🔄 Post-Redesign / Migration Gaps Websites recently redesigned or migrated often carry legacy redirect errors or broken link silos that damage search trust.
  • 📈 Rapidly Scaling Pages Adding hundreds of new pages requires a clean crawl pipeline to ensure search spiders discover and index new content quickly.
  • 🐢 Core Web Vitals Failure Slow loading speeds, high layout shifts, and poor mobile rendering directly penalize rankings on Google's mobile-first index.

Our Technical Audit Process

We analyze and diagnose your domain through a structured, multi-step pipeline.

STEP 01

Crawl & Diagnose

We run a deep, server-level crawl replicating search engine spider behaviors to discover hidden errors, broken paths, and indexing anomalies.

STEP 02

Infrastructure Review

We analyze your database load speeds, server headers, mobile layout responsiveness, render pathways, and SSL certificates.

STEP 03

Issue Prioritization

We rank all discovered errors by technical severity, estimating the direct impact resolving them will have on your traffic flow.

STEP 04

Actionable Plan

We provide a detailed document containing direct, step-by-step technical instructions for developers to implement fixes.
