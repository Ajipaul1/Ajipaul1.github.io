What Is a Technical SEO Audit?

A technical SEO audit examines how search engines access, crawl, and interpret your website. It focuses on the infrastructure that supports all SEO and content efforts.

Even strong content cannot rank if technical issues prevent search engines from understanding or trusting the website.

What We Review in a Technical SEO Audit

  • Search engine crawlability and site architecture
  • Indexing and canonicalization issues
  • Page speed and core web performance
  • Mobile usability and rendering problems
  • Broken links, redirects, and error pages
  • Structured data and technical signals
  • Security and HTTPS consistency

Who Needs a Technical SEO Audit?

  • Websites with traffic drops or unstable rankings
  • Businesses after a website redesign or migration
  • Sites with slow performance or indexing delays
  • Companies scaling SEO across many pages

Our Technical SEO Audit Process

  • Technical crawl and diagnostics
  • Infrastructure and performance review
  • Issue prioritization by impact
  • Clear explanations and action steps
