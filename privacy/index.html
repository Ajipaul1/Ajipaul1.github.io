Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

At TechAuditPros, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the confidential information of our clients. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you request a website audit or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when requesting a technical SEO audit, including your name, email address, phone number, and website URL. We also inspect public files and configuration data of the website URL provided to deliver the audit report.

We use the collected data strictly to analyze your website, compile the technical audit findings, communicate recommendation roadmaps to you, and send periodic updates about our services.

All audits, diagnostic outputs, and business contact information are stored securely on our systems. We do not sell, rent, or lease your website data or contact information to any third parties.
