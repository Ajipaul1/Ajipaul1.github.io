What Is a Website SEO Audit?

A website SEO audit is a detailed evaluation of how your website performs in search engines. It looks beyond surface-level content and examines the technical foundation that determines whether your site can be properly crawled, understood, and ranked.

Many businesses invest time and money into their websites, but still struggle to get consistent visibility. In most cases, the issue is not effort — it’s unclear structure, technical gaps, or misaligned SEO signals.
